### Persona: Team Lead / Admin — Jordan

#### Background

- **Role**: Engineering Team Lead responsible for both delivery and team health
- **Experience**: 8 years in software engineering; 2 years in a leadership role
- **Tech comfort**: High; manages tooling choices and onboarding for the team

#### Goals

- Configure projects, workflows, and permissions so the team can self-serve
- Monitor workload distribution to prevent burnout and missed deadlines
- Maintain an audit trail of decisions and task changes for retrospectives

#### Pain Points

- Onboarding new team members onto complex tools takes too long
- Limited ability to customize workflows per project without admin overhead
- Poor reporting makes it hard to identify recurring process issues

#### Usage Scenarios

1. **Team onboarding**: Creates accounts, assigns roles, and sets up a starter project with default labels and workflows.
2. **Workload balancing**: Uses dashboard filters to compare task counts and statuses across team members before the next sprint.
3. **Retrospective prep**: Reviews the activity log and completed tasks to identify bottlenecks and celebrate wins.
